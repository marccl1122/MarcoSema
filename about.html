Cornerstone College offers a dynamic, modern, and multicultural environment that caters to the diverse needs of students.

Our facilities are meticulously designed to provide a pleasant space for each student, mitigating the impact of being in a foreign country while upholding Canada’s high standard of educational quality.

Since 1980, Cornerstone College has been crafting a unique curriculum, delivering an exceptional experience through accredited Diploma Programs, Courses, and English as a Second Language classes.

At Cornerstone, our students benefit from access to a dedicated staff, ready to assist them from the moment they decide to join us until they are fully established, and throughout their academic journey until graduation.

Moving to a new country is a dream for many, and at Cornerstone, we are committed to making that dream unforgettable. Our constant progress revolves around providing the best experience for each student, ensuring their journey with us is both enriching and memorable.

Stating that “Your success is our priority” is not just a slogan but a guiding principle we hold dear. In the words of a renowned Canadian politician, ‘We welcome people from all nations to come in and share.”

MISSION

Our mission is to actively engage students in high-quality academic studies and practical work experiences. We are dedicated to fostering a multicultural environment that places a primary focus on individual success. Through innovative teaching methods, diverse learning opportunities, and personalized support, we aim to empower students to thrive academically and professionally in an ever-evolving global landscape. At the core of our mission is the commitment to creating an inclusive and dynamic educational community that prepares students for success in their chosen fields

LOCATION

Our Campuses

Main Campus / Management Campus 609 W Hastings St., Vancouver The ESL Campus is conveniently located at 543 Seymour St., Vancouver, BC, close to the Granville Skytrain Station. Just an 8-minute walk from the Vancouver Public Library, it is located in the heart of Vancouver, between the other two CICCC campuses. This central location ensures easy accessibility for students.
